About Me


My name is Grace Smart, I'm a student at San Jose State University, and I create designs which pose a challenge for me, and allow me to learn through creation. My goal in every project is to be more knowledgeable about the craft of design. Designs are varied and unique to allow practice in various aspects of design. Video games are a big passion of mine, and several of my designs are based on them.
